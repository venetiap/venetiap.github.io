Explainability cards and a new explanation lifecycle.

---

In the position "We need to explain our explanations," we take a deep look into how Shapley value explanations are currently produced, discuss the pitfalls of the current approaches, and introduce several new concepts to address the issue of having multiple concurrent valid explanations for the same model and instance.

We discuss explanation multiplicity [1] and show how it can be expresed as a parmetrization problem. We 

how it poses both a threat and an opportunity for explainability. We issue a call to action, and introduce a new explanation lifecycle, a novel unified Shapley value definition, and Shapley value explainability cards!
